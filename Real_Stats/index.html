Real Stats

During the Coughacks Hack-a-Thon, our team of five student programmers had only 24 hours to create functional software based on a surprise theme.We researched, planned, designed, developed, tested, and presented our project within this tight timeframe.

Our project, "Real Stats," is a unique project inspired by classic game character stat sheets. It helps users track their progress and set achievable objectives for self-improvement and overall wellness. We made daunting tasks more manageable by breaking them into smaller steps, adding an element of fun and motivation.
